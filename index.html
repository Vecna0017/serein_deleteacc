Delete Your Serein Account

At Serein, we respect your privacy. You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data, including:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Install date
  • Last used timestamp

To delete your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Serein app on your device.
  2. Go to your Profile screen.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Alternatively, if you cannot access the app, you can email us at cygnusxofficial@gmail.com with the subject: Serein Account Deletion Request. We will process your request as soon as possible.

Note: Once deleted, your personal data is permanently removed from our servers. Some anonymous analytics data may be retained for app improvement purposes only.

Thank you for using Serein.
